# User Personas & Target Audience Profile

## Primary audience

StreamYolo targets creators who host live rooms and viewers who want interactive, token-based participation and paid private access.

## Persona 1: Creator / Performer

### Goals

- Go live quickly.
- Control room appearance before viewers enter.
- Earn from tips and private sessions.
- Know what viewers see.
- Manage chat without feeling overwhelmed.
- Convert public viewers into paid private sessions.

### Needs

- Strong creator studio.
- Required go-live checklist.
- Current public thumbnail preview.
- Tip menu builder.
- Room goal controls.
- Private rules/pricing setup.
- Clear private queue.
- Visible session earnings.

### Friction points

- Bad thumbnails reduce conversion.
- Chat can become noisy.
- Generic tips lack intent.
- Private requests need balance verification.
- Creator must trust the platform not to expose private-session imagery publicly.

## Persona 2: Viewer / Participant

### Goals

- Discover rooms quickly.
- Understand how to participate.
- Chat, tip, and request private access.
- Use tokens without feeling confused or scammed.
- Know private-session price and time estimate before committing.

### Needs

- Simple room browse.
- Clear token balance.
- Fast token purchase.
- Obvious chat/tip/private controls.
- Tip menu with specific request amounts.
- Visible room goal.
- Trustworthy wallet ledger.

### Friction points

- Payment trust.
- Unclear token conversion.
- Unclear private-session cost.
- Room empty or awkward without prompts.

## Persona 3: Admin / Operator

### Goals

- Understand what is happening on the site right now.
- Keep real-money flows correct.
- Intervene in problematic rooms.
- Handle reports, media, payments, chargebacks, and wallet issues.
- Operate manually during early POC.

### Needs

- Admin overview.
- Live room list.
- Room detail with chat/tips/private events.
- Payment transaction viewer.
- Wallet ledger with append-only adjustments.
- User/creator suspension.
- Media review/hide.
- Reports queue.

## Target usage mindset

Viewers are not just watching; they are participating. Creators are not just broadcasting; they are managing a room economy.

## Audience positioning

StreamYolo should feel more like a live room control platform than a traditional cam-site clone. The product appeal is fast rooms, low chrome, clear participation, creator control, and immediate room feedback.
